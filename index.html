About Afiaprime Lab

Afiaprime Lab is a non-profit organization based in Lagos, Nigeria, dedicated to empowering the next generation of tech leaders across Africa.

Our mission is to train 10,000 young people in the fields of cybersecurity, data analysis, and software development over the next decade.

Our Vision

We envision Afiaprime Lab becoming one of the largest and most impactful tech hubs in Africa.

Our Mission

At Afiaprime Lab, our mission is to provide comprehensive training and mentorship to 10,000 young people over the next 10 years.

Our Approach

We believe that access to quality education and resources should not be limited by location or financial circumstance.
