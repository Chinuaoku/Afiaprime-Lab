About Afiaprime Lab

Afiaprime Lab is a non-profit organization based in Lagos, Nigeria, dedicated to empowering the next generation of tech leaders across Africa. Our mission is to train 10,000 young people in the fields of cybersecurity, data analysis, and software development over the next decade. As a hub of innovation and skill development, we are passionate about fostering hands-on learning experiences for young individuals starting from scratch in the world of technology.

Our Vision

We envision Afiaprime Lab becoming one of the largest and most impactful tech hubs in Africa. We aim to create a thriving ecosystem where young Africans, particularly those from underserved communities, can learn, grow, and thrive in the digital world. By providing the resources and support needed to develop technical skills, we strive to empower individuals to shape the future of the continent and contribute to its growing digital economy.

Our Mission

At Afiaprime Lab, our mission is simple but bold: to provide comprehensive training and mentorship to 10,000 young people over the next 10 years. We are committed to providing the tools and knowledge necessary for these individuals to succeed in the fields of cybersecurity, data analysis, and software development. Through a combination of structured training programs, real-world projects, and mentorship, we aim to equip learners with the practical skills needed to excel in today's rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Our Approach

We believe that access to quality education and resources should not be limited by location or financial circumstance. Afiaprime Lab offers a unique, inclusive approach to learning that focuses on the following:

  • Accessible Education: We provide free and subsidized training programs designed for beginners, ensuring that anyone, regardless of their background, can embark on a tech career. Our curriculum is hands-on and designed to give learners practical experience that prepares them for real-world challenges.
  • Support for Innovation: We understand that talent is not limited to the classroom.   • Job Readiness and Opportunities: Our connections with tech industry leaders and employers allow us to link learners with job opportunities, internships, and freelance work. We provide career guidance and practical experience through collaborations with tech companies and startups, enabling learners to gain hands-on experience that makes them job-ready.
  • Community and Collaboration: We foster a vibrant, supportive community where learners can collaborate, network, and share knowledge. We believe in the power of mentorship, peer learning, and collaboration to drive innovation and success.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to ensuring the long-term success of our programs by maintaining a sustainable model supported by donations, partnerships, and collaborations. We accept all forms of donations, including funding, technology, and mentorship, to help support the continued growth of Afiaprime Lab and its learners.
